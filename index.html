Burger Nation

Welcome to Burger Nation.

Here, you can customize your own burger by selecting from a variety of toppings. Simply click on the toppings you want to add or remove from your burger. We offer a range of options, including tomato, lettuce, onion, pickle, cheese, hamburger, ketchup, mustard, and mayo.

At the bottom of the page, you'll see a section that displays the total price of your burger, which updates as you add or remove toppings.

When you're ready to pay, simply click on the "Pay" button.

I've also included a navigation menu at the top of the page, where you can easily access the homepage, visualizer, and FAQ.

I hope you enjoy using the burger visualizer and creating your own perfect burger!
